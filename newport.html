About

Newport is a beauitful beach city located in southeast Rhode Island. Newport has a lot to offer. It's a well-known and popular vacation destination, and has been for decades. Newport was founded in 1639, but wasn't incorporated until 1784 (City, 2023). It is an old city with rich history. There are buildings and structures that still stand from around the time Newport was founded. One being the Newport Tower, built in around 1670. Another example is the oldest tavern in all of America, White Horse Tavern (Wikimedia, 2024a)!


Today, Newport holds all of that history and reamins a bustling city. It is known for holding the first US Open tournaments for golf and tennis (Wikimedia, 2024c). It is also known for the beautiful beaches.
