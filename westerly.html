About

Westerly is a small beach town located in southwest Rhode Island that borders Connecticut. It is about 10 minutes to Connecticut by car. It was first founded in 1661 and then incorporated in 1669 (Wikimedia, 2023). This town may be small, but it has a lot to offer! From beaches to restaurants to shopping, this is the perfect place to go for a day trip. This small town has scenic views from everywhere.


Westerly is full of small villages. Each of these villages have neighborhood communities, restaurants, and shopping. Most shopping options are small local owned businesses.

If you like the small-town feel while still feeling like you're on vacation, Westerly is the place for you!
