About

Providence, one of the oldest cities in all of New Enlgand, is located in the northeast region of Rhode Island. It is a beauitful city full of history. It was settled in 1636 but wasn't incorporated until November 5, 1832 (Encyclop√¶dia, 2024). Being one of New England's first cities, it is home to many historic homes and buildings. What is now the Visitors' Center at Roger Williams National Memorial, was a house built in 1736 (Wikimedia, 2024a). This house is older than America! Another historic building in Providence is University Hall. It was built in 1770 and is the oldest academic building in all of Rhode Island (Wikimedia, 2024a). Today, Providence is the most populated city in Rhode Island. There are 8 colleges and universities in Providence alone, inlcuding Brown University and Rhode Island School of Design (Wikimedia, 2024b).
